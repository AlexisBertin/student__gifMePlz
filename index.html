For this experimence, you will be asked to put on your best facial expressions in reaction to the words appearing on your screen.

There will be 8 words, all related to emotions such as “sadness” or “happiness”.

They will be displayed one at a time and a picture will automatically be taken before the next word appears.

Once we have the 8 shots, you will get the chance to make a gif out of them and, if you want, share it on the gifMePlz twitter's account.

The camera block should appear above this text.

You will have to grant access to your camera to this website (pop-up window at the top of the browser page).

Once your face appears on the video block, you are all set for the experiment to begin. You just have to press the START button and enjoy.
